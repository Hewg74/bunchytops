## 1. Castaway

### Vibe
Escapist, sun-drenched, freedom-seeking. The title evokes being intentionally lost — choosing disconnection over conformity. Fits the band's stated theme of "rejecting societal constraints." Think: feet in the sand, cold drink, no phone.

### Tone
Reflective but not heavy. Laid-back delivery with an undercurrent of restlessness — the "east coast grit" layered under island warmth. Not party reggae; more introspective island rock.

### Sound Profile
- **Genre blend:** Reggae rhythms + rock guitar energy (think Slightly Stoopid meets Sublime meets Stick Figure)
- **Instrumentation (likely):** Clean/overdriven guitar, reggae-skank rhythm guitar, bass-heavy groove, organic drums
- **Production:** Indie-level — raw and honest rather than polished pop-reggae

### Target Demographic
- **Age:** 18-35
- **Profile:** Surf/outdoor culture, Hawaii locals & transplants, mainland reggae-rock fans, Sublime/Rebelution/Stick Figure listeners
- **Platforms:** Spotify Discovery, reggae-rock playlists, Hawaii local radio, Instagram Reels with ocean/adventure content

### Energy Level
**Medium (5-6/10)** — Groovy and steady, not high-energy mosh pit material. Sway-worthy, drive-with-windows-down energy.

### Mood Tags
`chill` `freedom` `island` `introspective` `sun-soaked` `indie`

## 2. North Side

### Vibe
Place-rooted, nostalgic, identity-driven. "North Side" signals a specific geography — likely Maui's north shore or an east coast reference tying back to the band's roots. Sense of belonging and origin story.

### Tone
Grittier and more grounded than Castaway. The "east coast" influence likely comes through stronger here — more rock edge, more directness in the lyrics. Hometown pride meets island life.

### Sound Profile
- **Genre blend:** Heavier on the rock side of reggae-rock. More driving rhythm, possibly uptempo compared to Castaway.
- **Instrumentation (likely):** Distorted guitar sections, punchy drums, reggae verses with rock choruses
- **Production:** Same indie rawness — authentic over polished

### Target Demographic
- **Age:** 18-35
- **Profile:** Same core audience as Castaway but appeals more to rock-leaning listeners. Local Maui scene, people who identify with "where I'm from" anthems.
- **Platforms:** Same channels, plus potential crossover into indie rock playlists

### Energy Level
**Medium-High (6-7/10)** — More momentum and drive than Castaway. Head-nodding, live-show energy.

### Mood Tags
`roots` `gritty` `hometown` `rock-edge` `authentic` `pride`
