## Bio
Rooted in reggae and soaked in rock energy, The Bunchy Tops bring island energy with east coast grit. The group evolved from songwriter Charlie Ward into a full Maui-based ensemble. Their music addresses themes of personal freedom and rejecting societal constraints, delivered through honest lyrics and laid-back grooves.
